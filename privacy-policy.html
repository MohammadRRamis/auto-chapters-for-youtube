Auto Chapters for YouTube

Privacy Policy

This extension is designed to work locally in your browser. We do not collect, sell, or share your personal data, and we do not operate any backend service that receives your browsing data or generated chapters.

Effective date: April 22, 2026

What We Collect

We do not collect personal data.

The extension does not require account registration, does not run its own analytics, and does not send your information to any developer-controlled server.

How The Extension Works

When you use the extension on a YouTube video without native chapters, it can prepare a prompt for Gemini, open Gemini in a new tab, and store the current request state and generated chapter data in your browser's extension storage so the feature works correctly.

  • The stored data stays locally in your browser.
  • No remote database or developer-owned server is used by this project.
  • The extension only uses the minimum browser permission needed for local storage.

Third-Party Services

This extension may open and interact with Gemini as part of the chapter-generation workflow. If you choose to use that workflow, your use of Gemini is subject to Google's own terms and privacy policy, not this extension's policy.

This extension does not control how third-party services process data once you are on their websites.

Data Sharing

We do not sell your data. We do not rent your data. We do not transfer your data to advertisers, data brokers, or analytics providers.

Changes To This Policy

If the extension's data practices change in the future, this page will be updated before those changes take effect.
